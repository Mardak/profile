Firefox Personalization Study

Mozilla Firefox is conducting a study of personalized content on the Web, and as a Test Pilot user, we'd really appreciate your help in completing this study.

Mozilla Firefox is conducting a study of personalized content on the Web. To participate, please review the information below.

How it works

Firefox will choose a set of interest topics (such as music, humor, or technology) that best match the websites you tend to visit, based on your browsing history. You will then be asked to answer some questions about those topics and your browsing preferences.

These interests and study data will only be shared with Mozilla, unless you authorize specific websites to access your interests (view a sample of the data). This study period lasts 6 months, after which it will be removed from your computer.

Please review this study's Frequently Asked Questions and Privacy Policy for more information.
